Hi! My name is Sam. I am a proud momma of 4 kiddos and the owner of a fashion boutique called Zuchinni Threads. It's named after my love for Zuchinni and in honor of my mother, Zinnia who loved fashion as well. I don't know how to sew but I sure know how to shop so I find our beautiful clothing from a range of high quality manufacturers. I hand pick collections for every season and in every size! We also sell shoes, jewelry, oddities & various home decor items.

In my early years I worked as a teacher and insurance representative. After having kids I realized I needed to find a career path that would support our family's needs better. I am a creative person at heart we took a chance and opened up shop! With the rise of technology I found that online was where we needed to be in order to grow our business. Today I am working towards completing my certification as a full stack web developer in hopes of learning the ropes and obtaining a career in front end development. Once i'm able to improve my craft I plan to start a website for our business!

Thank you for learning about me!
